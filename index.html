Pondering Reality is a short VR puzzle game experience which puts you in the role of a wizard who wishes to travel to the future to look at cat pictures. A truly noble goal.

    Skills I learnt during development

  • C# unity programming - learnt a lot about how scripts interact with each other both in code and inside the unity inspector. Code Repository

  • VR Game Design - designing features such as the cauldron puzzle where you have to combine different items to produce a desired outcome, find/create all 5 pentagram items and the fix the computer puzzle. These challenges were much harder to due constraints such as being unable move your legs and designing with VR equipment in mind.

  • Videogame Music Production - before this project I only had experience with normal music production; e.g. making a full length song. Videogame music is much different due creating repeatable loops opposed to linear songs.

I produced music loops for this game, one for each level. Wizard Loop plays while you are in the tower and Chiptune Loop while in the future.
